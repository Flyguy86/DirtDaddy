Product Details

The Dirt Daddy is a brand new American invention. It lines the floor of your truck bed with a custom tarp and then pulls the tarp off the vehicle. Think of it as a massive paper towel roll that pulls your truck load off the truck bed.

An electric winch powers the device. Suitable for gravel, debris, mulch, dirt, household dumps, etc. Suitable for automating city dump runs, DIY home improvement, or landscaping and gardening projects.

Our rugged steel design and innovative simple design ensures the tool operates under the toughest conditions.
